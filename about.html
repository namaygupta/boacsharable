About our clinic

Healing the community, together.

The Open Arms Health Clinic provides free medical care to those who are uninsured or underinsured. We believe everyone deserves access to compassionate, high-quality healthcare.

Our Core Services

We provide comprehensive gap care to meet the immediate needs of our community.

Education & Care

The Open Arms Student-Run Free Clinic

Developed by a group of Boonshoft School of Medicine Medical Students, the Student-Run Free Clinic exists to provide care for the uninsured and underinsured in the Dayton area while providing a vital educational experience for medical students.

The clinic is entirely run by medical students and strictly overseen by a licensed physician.

Location & Walk-ins

We are located at 4403 OH-725 Suite E, Bellbrook, OH.
Walk-in appointments are available on the first Saturday of every month from 9 a.m. - 1 p.m.

Paths to Wellness Mission

"Paths to Wellness is an inter-professional group of students dedicated to promoting the health and well-being of members of the surrounding community. We counsel patients through individualized, integrative sessions on health-related topics such as nutrition, stress management, sleep, movement and exercise. Together, we envision a future of healthcare that is as dedicated to the promotion of health and well-being as it is to the curing and healing of disease."

Holistic Health

What is Paths to Wellness?

A unique program run by Wright State medical and psychology students at the Open Arms Clinic. Every second Saturday of the month, we offer free, private sessions to help answer questions about your health and learn simple changes for a healthier lifestyle. We even offer online appointments through Doxy.me, a HIPAA-compliant telehealth platform for those with barriers to transportation.

We also offer free community wellness workshops once a month at 9:30 a.m. at the BellHOP cafe.

What can it teach me?

Whether you want to learn how to get better sleep or simple ways to reduce stress, our students are trained to provide counseling on stress reduction, nutrition, exercise, sleep, and other health-related topics. Come talk with us—we are here to assist you!

What is it NOT?

Paths to Wellness is NOT a medical clinic. We will not be diagnosing or treating any medical conditions during these sessions. If you need to see a doctor for a medical problem, please visit our medical clinic on Tuesday evenings.

How can I participate?

Call our office at (937) 848-2939 to schedule an appointment. 1-hour appointments are available from 9 am to noon (in-person or online via Doxy) on the second Saturday of every month. Our receptionist is available to schedule on Tuesday evenings and every first and third Friday.
