Clinic Information

Bellbrook Open Arms Clinic is a Gap Clinic. This means we are designed to bridge the gap in healthcare for individuals who are uninsured, underinsured, or currently between health coverage plans. We exist to provide essential, routine care to those who cannot afford it elsewhere.

Please Note:

  • We are staffed by dedicated volunteers and operate on specific days and hours.
  • We do not have the equipment or staff to handle traumatic injuries, heart attacks, strokes, or life-threatening emergencies.
  • Services are provided on a walk-in basis or by appointment, depending on the specific clinic day.
